Taster Application

Prototype in Adobe XD 

What is the TASTER?

In the future, our life is more dependent on the high technic products, we use smart devices every day. Social media also become the most part of our lives, but do you have more need from that? My answer is YES! I am a food lover, always explore a new place to eat. What if the pics you took about food or drink also come with the taste, so you can share it with everyone?

 My product is an app that you can download from the online app store to your smart devices, also it comes along with accessories smart bags, and smart dissoluble organic paper, which you can order from our website. The smart bags that you can put the food or drink in, plug with your devices, download the taste to your app.

Then you can upload a picture of the food with the taste. For whoever saw the pictures online about the food or drinks, and wondering how they taste like, you can just plug the smart paper with your devices, click the pictures, download the taste to the paper, and put in your mouth, now you have the taste of the food.

Our product can use by any restaurant menus, social media apps, such as Instagram, Facebook, etc., or food takeout apps, such as yelp, seamless, grab hub, etc. But, for the taste, one account is only allowed to use once on one picture per day.

So, would you like to become a TASTER?
