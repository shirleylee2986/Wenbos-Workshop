Fort Greene Farmers Market

STYLE GUIDE & GRAPHIC USAGE STANDARDS

Primary Identity

Located outside the entrance of historic Fort Greene Park and surrounded by chestnut trees and centuries-old brownstones, the bustling Fort Greene market serves a diverse community of young, urban professionals and multi-generational Fort Greene families who gather every Saturday, year-round, to mingle and shop for everything from micro greens to affordable staples. The idea of the design is to hope that local residents can enjoy natural and healthy food and living environment. The building under design is a landmark in the park.
